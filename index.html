My dissertation involves adaptive systems and applications of learning models to real-life scenarios. In my free time, I love travelling (when I can afford it), learning about history and running on sunny days!

what's this website?

I made this website both as a test of my 1st year CSS & HTML skills, but also to form a portfolio for various data science projects I've completed. In my opinion, 5 lines on a cv is simply not enough! I'll also be experimenting with some front-end designs and trying to blend a bit of data science into this website, again as a personal challenge!

Also, as someone who really digs online to understand concepts, I've collated some of my favourite resources on the web for: Machine Learning, Data Science, Data Visualization (SQL!) and Artificial Intelligence. I'm hoping whoever stumbles on here (maybe some other students) might find the resources helpful!

Finally, I use the Feynman Technique a lot in my studies, and so I've made my own Medium-inspired articles regarding some of my final year modules - these are solely for my own studying, but feel free to check those out too. They have explanations but also data visualization, some modelling and mathematics in there.
